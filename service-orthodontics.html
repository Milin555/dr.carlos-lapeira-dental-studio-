Orthodontics

Straighten Your Teeth, Align Your Future

Properly aligned teeth look beautiful and function better, reducing wear and making hygiene easier. Our orthodontic department offers customized solutions for children, teens, and adults, using standard braces or modern clear aligners to correct crowding, gaps, and bite issues.

Our Alignment Options

Clear Aligners (Invisalign)

Virtually invisible, removable plastic trays that gradually nudge your teeth into alignment without wire adjustments or eating restrictions.

Modern Traditional Braces

Low-profile metal or ceramic brackets coupled with advanced archwires, providing powerful correction for complex structural crowding or bite alignments.

Bite Correction & Jaw Alignment

Therapy plans dedicated to fixing overbites, underbites, crossbites, and open bites, resolving jaw joint tension (TMJ) and improving speech.

Retainers & Follow-Up Care

Maintain your perfect, newly aligned smile with custom retainers and minor periodic checkups after active treatment wraps up.
