Dr. Neofytos Ioannides

Chief Dentist & Implant Specialist

Dr. Neofytos Ioannides is the chief dentist at Dr. Neo Boutique Dental Clinic. He obtained his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from the renowned University of Leipzig, Germany. To further enhance his skills in restorative, cosmetic dentistry, and surgical procedures, he completed a post-graduate advanced residency in general dentistry. Since establishing the Boutique Dental Clinic, Dr. Neo has expanded his practice to incorporate digital dentistry and implant technology, offering his patients the most advanced and comprehensive dental care available in Limassol, Cyprus.

Background & Philosophy

Dr. Neo believes that a healthy smile starts with precision and care. He specializes in providing advanced aesthetic treatments, teeth whitening, and general dentistry, ensuring high precision and optimal patient comfort.

For all patients, he applies state-of-the-art restorative techniques, including porcelain veneers, non-invasive whitening, and full smile rehabilitation, to achieve natural-looking, beautiful results without years of discomfort.
